Privacy Policy — Skedule

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Skedule helps you create calendar events from appointment screenshots you share to the app.

What data Skedule processes

  • When you share a screenshot to the Skedule Share Extension, Skedule processes the image on your device to detect appointment details (e.g., date/time, and optionally a location or meeting link).

What Skedule does NOT do

  • Skedule does not upload your screenshots or extracted text to any server.
  • Skedule does not store your screenshots or extracted text.
  • Skedule does not sell your data.

Calendar access

Skedule requests calendar access only to create events you choose to save. You can manage permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

Diagnostics

If you have iOS analytics/crash reporting enabled on your device, Apple may provide anonymized crash logs to help improve app stability.
